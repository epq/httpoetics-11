About Wulianmao

Wulianmao is a delightful orange mackerel tabby cat living his best life in Taichung, Taiwan. Known for his spirited personality and advocacy for safety, he brings fuzzy feelings to the environment around him.

Wulianmao's Favourite Activities

  • Assuming the famous "bean pose" on his favourite red chair. Or more recently, curled up in his Bubble Milk Tea box in his cubby.
  • Playing with his collection of toys (worms!)
  • Exploring the safe corners of his domain
  • Observing the world from the windowsill
